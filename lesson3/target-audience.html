Target Audience

The audience of for this site will be business people who are looking to align themselves with a supportive and active group. This is for businesses who want a group that hosts events for charities and to raise awareness of the small businesses in our community.

Personas

First

The first group of people will be small businesses. Generally they will be family owned and operated with some outside help. Their business will not be known to most of the population in the area so they will need to be recognized. Joining this chamber will help them with that.

Second

The second group of people will be franchises and bigger, more stable businesses. They will be looking to align themselves with a group where they will be able to receive support as well as be the support to the chamber

Scenarios

  1. What are the benefits of joining this chamber?
  2. What areas do they service?
  3. Will they help advertise my business if I join?
  4. When are the upcoming events?
  5. What do they require from me as a member?
